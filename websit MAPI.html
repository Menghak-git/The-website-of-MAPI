The Story of MAPI Bakery

MAPI started as a passionate dream in a small kitchen, dedicated to preserving traditional French viennoiserie. Founded by master baker Marc-Antoine and pastry chef Isabella (the initials of which create MAPI), we embarked on a relentless quest to source the world’s best ingredients.

Today, while our bakery has evolved into a modern luxury experience, our foundational recipe remains untouched: slow fermentation, pure high-fat Normandy butter, French flour from the grandest mills, and unconditional love for the crust.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know about our premium croissants.

How do I reheat my croissants to get that fresh-baked crunch?

Preheat your oven or toaster oven to 175°C (350°F). Place the croissants on a baking sheet and bake for 3 to 4 minutes. Do NOT microwave them, as this will make the butter soft and doughy instead of delightfully crispy.

What makes AOP French Butter so special?

AOP (Appellation d'Origine Protégée) is a strict European certification. It guarantees the milk comes from protected cow pastures in Charentes-Poitou or Normandy. This butter has a unique elasticity, higher fat percentage (82%), and a rich hazelnut aroma perfect for creating thousands of delicate laminate layers.

Do you offer local delivery / catering for corporate events?

Yes, indeed! We cater to corporate meetups, weddings, and premium breakfasts with special custom boxes and catering servers. Please write to events@mapi-bakery.com or complete our contact form.
